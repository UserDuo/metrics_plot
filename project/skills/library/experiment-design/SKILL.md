# Experiment Design

## Checklist
- Sampling strategy and representativeness
- Randomization and counterbalancing
- Control conditions and baselines
- Measurement validity and reliability
- Power analysis (if applicable)
- Pre-registration considerations

## Protocol Output
- Objective and hypotheses
- Materials and stimuli
- Procedure and randomization
- Metrics and analysis plan
- Inclusion/exclusion criteria
